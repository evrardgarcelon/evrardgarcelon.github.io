Evrard Garcelon

 

I am a first-year PhD student jointly at FAIR Paris and CREST, ENSAE. I am co-advised by Matteo Pirotta and Vianney Perchet. Contact: evrard dot garcelon at gmail dot com .

Research

My main research interest is Reinforcement Learning (RL), in particular theoretical aspects of exploration, privacy and security of RL.
